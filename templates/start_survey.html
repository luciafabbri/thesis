Welcome to the Comments Survey

Welcome! This survey is part of a thesis project that aims to study the toxicity of online comments. Your participation will help us better understand how people perceive and classify toxic comments.

In the following screen, you will be asked to read a comment (taken out of context) and evaluate its toxicity. You will rate it on a scale: Non-toxic, A little toxic, Toxic, and Very toxic.

We will also ask you to provide your age and gender for statistical purposes. Your responses will be kept anonymous.
